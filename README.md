# Green Path Tracker 🌿

Welcome to the Green Path Tracker! This application helps you monitor your carbon footprint, log daily eco-friendly habits, and watch your virtual village thrive as you make sustainable choices.

## 🚀 Live App

**[Click here to open the Green Path Tracker App!](https://1306shivamchauhan-cpu.github.io/green-path-tracker/)**

## Features
- **Carbon Footprint Calculator**: Estimate your weekly carbon emissions.
- **Village Dashboard**: Watch your village grow and flourish based on your sustainable actions.
- **Habit Tracker**: Log daily pledges to earn leaves and unlock badges.
- **AI Simulator**: An advanced predictive simulation for optimizing environmental impact.
